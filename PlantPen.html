The Problem: A Plastic Predicament

Every year, billions of conventional pens, made almost entirely of plastic, end up in landfills. These pens take centuries to decompose, contributing significantly to plastic pollution that harms our environment and wildlife.

  • 95% Plastic: Conventional pens are overwhelmingly plastic.
  • Centuries to Decompose: They persist in the environment for generations.
  • Niche "Eco" Alternatives: Current plantable options lack robust design, controlled biodegradability, and academic validation.

There's a critical gap in the market for a scalable, truly sustainable stationery solution that combines advanced materials science, effective seed encapsulation, and comprehensive lifecycle analysis.

Our Solution: PlantPen+

We're developing an advanced, holistic stationery ecosystem designed to be truly biodegradable and plantable, transforming waste into life.

How PlantPen+ Works

Innovative Design & Materials

Our pens are engineered for sustainability from the ground up. We utilize cutting-edge materials and design principles to ensure both functionality and environmental responsibility.

  • Biodegradable Composites: Made from paper or agri-waste polymers.
  • Integrated Seed Capsule: Securely embedded in the pen's base, protecting the seeds until planting.
  • Enhanced Refill System: Vegetable-oil coating prevents ink diffusion, ensuring an 18-month shelf life.
  • Eco-Conscious Packaging: Designed to be planter-friendly, completing the lifecycle.

Our Prototyping Strategy

Phase 1: Bench Trials

Initial experiments with 3 materials and 2 seed types. Focus on ink compatibility and early degradation testing in controlled environments.

Phase 2: Pilot Batch

Production of 100 units for comprehensive user testing in real-world scenarios like colleges and co-working spaces.

Phase 3: Feedback Loop & Refinement

Collecting and analyzing user feedback to refine the seed mix, optimize shelf life, and prepare for scaling.
